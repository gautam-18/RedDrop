Donate Blood & Save a Life

Donating blood is a selfless act because it involves giving up one's own blood to help someone else inneed.It is an act of kindness and generosity that does not require any monetary compensation. Donating bloodcan save lives, and it is a way to give back to the community and help those in need. It is an act ofcompassion and selflessness that can make a real difference in someone's life.

About Us

REDDROP is a platform which fills the gap between blood donors and receivers who are in need Users can register as a donor or as a normal user . Hospitals can use the platform to find out which blood bank have the required units available and collect those units as needed.
